CONTROLS:

W: Move forward

S: Move backward

A: Move leftward

D: Move rightward

E: Equip fishing pole

SpaceBar: Catch a fish

Mouse: Adjust the view

GAME:

- Once equipped the fishing pole, move the robot and try to catch some fish!

- When a fish touches the fishing line, try to catch it. If you succeed, +1 point.

Otherwise, -1 point.

END:

- You win when the lake is void

- You lose when you reach -4 points
